Travel Safety Intelligence

Essential safety information for travelers. Stay aware of risks, scams, and emergency contacts while visiting unfamiliar places.

🛡 Safety Tips

  • Avoid isolated areas at night
  • Keep copies of important documents
  • Use official taxis or trusted ride apps
  • Beware of pickpockets in crowded areas

⚠ Common Tourist Scams

  • Fake taxi drivers overcharging tourists
  • Street distraction scams
  • Fake police asking for passports
  • Currency exchange tricks

🚑 Emergency Numbers

  • Police — 100
  • Ambulance — 102
  • Fire — 101
